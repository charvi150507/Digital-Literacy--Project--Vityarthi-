Social Media Do’s and Dont's checklist 

✅ Do’s
- Use strong privacy settings
- Share useful and positive content
- Verify information before sharing
- Be respectful in comments and posts
- Use social media for learning and networking
- Keep your profile professional and updated
- Think before posting anything online
- Respect others’ opinions and privacy
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Follow credible and educational pages
- Use proper language and avoid slang
- Report inappropriate or harmful content
- Give credit when sharing others’ content
- Use social media for learning and networking

❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal information publicly
- Avoid posting inappropriate content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Do not spread fake news
- Don’t overshare personal details (phone, address, etc.)
- Don’t post when angry or emotional
- Avoid using offensive or abusive language
- Don’t trust unknown friend requests
- Avoid spending too much time on social media
- Don’t click on suspicious ads or links
- Don’t share fake or unverified information
- Avoid posting confidential academic/work content
- Don’t engage in online arguments or fights
- Don’t use weak passwords
